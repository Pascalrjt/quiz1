Culinary in Malang

Bakso

Perhaps bakso is what Malang is best known for. Bakso is a nice meatball soup with nice and rich broth.

Rawon Dengkul

Rawon dengkul is a nice and hearty stew that makes use of the beef knee. It also has a very nice and rich broth and you can enjoy it with the knee ligaments of the cow.

Tahu Campur

Tahu campur is a salad that contains tofu, bean sprouts, noodles and mustard greens, some are added with crackers and doused with a very delicious petis sauce.
