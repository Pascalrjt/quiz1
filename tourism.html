Tourist Locations

Museum Angkut

Museum Angkut is toursist spot for classic car lovers, history buffs and Hollywood movie buffs. With an extensive collection of vehicles and props you will find yourself at awe while exploring the location.

Jatim Park

Jatim Park is a famous tourist spot that is divided into three parts, namely Jatim Park 1, Jatim Park 2, and Jatim Park 3. Each has a different attractions.

Batu Night Spektakular

Stone Night Spectacular is a local tourist spot in stone that only operates at night. This tourist spot has attractions that highlight the night-vibe.
